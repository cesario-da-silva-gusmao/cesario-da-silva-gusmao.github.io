Historia Konaba IOB

East Timor Institute of Business (IOB) hanesan instituisaun ida ne’ebé ho kualidade no exelénsia ensinu superiór hari'i husi Fundação Klibur Mata Dalan Coopertiva no Fila Liman ne’ebé estabelese iha loron 20 fulan agostu tinan 2002 nia sede iha rua Praia dos Coqueiros ( ex-Pertamina) Dili, no ho konsentrasaun iha área ekonomia, no teknolojia informátika. IOB ne’e konsege pontuação ida ne’ebé aas tebe-tebes, iha nível nasionál no internasionál, iha akreditasaun ne’ebé governu RDTL liu husi ministériu edukasaun iha tinan 2009, ho baze iha avaliasaun ne’ebé halo husi Komisaun Internasional Independente nomeasaun ba Banku Mundiál, liu husi akadémiku 7 ho nasaun 6 ne’ebé mak la hanesan, no categorizadu liu iha mundu.East Timor Institute of Business (IOB) hanesan instituisaun ida ne’ebé ho kualidade no exelénsia ensinu superiór hari'i husi Fundação Klibur Mata Dalan Coopertiva no Fila Liman ne’ebé estabelese iha loron 20 fulan agostu tinan 2002 nia sede iha rua Praia dos Coqueiros ( ex-Pertamina) Dili, no ho konsentrasaun iha área ekonomia, no teknolojia informátika. Misaun IOB

IOB aims to become a leading centre of Higher Education Institution in Timor-Leste to guarantee comprehensive and preferred excellent scientific studies to produce professional human resources with knowledge, skills, attitude and professionalism for the well-being of the people and the country based on academic principles, norms and values



Visaun IOB

1.To be part of the government and its related institutions to develop the higher education institutions for the country.
2.To make utmost efforts to guarantee quality of higher education institutions in Timor-Leste.
3.To produce graduates with wide perspective of the nation, discipline, high moral standard and hardworking.
4.To produce human resources who are competent, professional and marketable.
